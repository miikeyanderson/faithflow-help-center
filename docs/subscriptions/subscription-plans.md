# Subscription Plans

Unlock premium features with a Faithflow subscription.

## Free vs. Premium

### Free Features
- Access to core lessons
- Basic progress tracking
- Limited hearts (refill over time)
- Daily streak tracking
- Community features

### Premium Features
- **Unlimited Hearts**: Never wait for hearts to refill
- **Ad-Free Experience**: No interruptions
- **Premium Content**: Exclusive lessons and materials
- **Priority Support**: Faster response times
- **Advanced Analytics**: Detailed progress insights
- **Offline Mode**: Download lessons for offline use

## Subscription Options

### Monthly Subscription
- Billed monthly
- Cancel anytime
- Immediate access to premium features

### Annual Subscription
- Billed annually
- Save up to 40% vs monthly
- Best value

### Lifetime Access
- One-time payment
- Permanent premium access
- All future features included

## Managing Your Subscription

### Subscribe
1. Tap **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Choose a plan
3. Confirm purchase via Apple App Store
4. Premium features activate immediately

### Cancel Subscription
1. iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
2. Select Faithflow
3. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation

**Note**: Access continues until the end of current billing period.

### Restore Purchases
If you reinstall the app or switch devices:
1. Settings → Subscription
2. Tap "Restore Purchases"
3. Sign in with the same Apple ID

## Billing

- All subscriptions are billed through the Apple App Store
- Pricing may vary by region
- Auto-renews unless canceled 24 hours before renewal
- Manage billing through your Apple ID

## Refunds

Refund requests are handled by Apple:
1. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Find your Faithflow purchase
4. Select "Request a Refund"

Alternatively, [contact our support team](../troubleshooting/contact-support) for assistance.
