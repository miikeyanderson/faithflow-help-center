# Getting Started Guide

Your first steps with Faithflow - learn the basics and start your spiritual journey.

## Welcome to Faithflow!

After creating your account, here's how to get the most out of Faithflow:

## 1. Complete Your First Lesson

1. Browse the **Lessons** tab
2. Select a lesson that interests you
3. Tap "Start Lesson" to begin
4. Complete activities to earn XP

## 2. Understand the Basics

### XP (Experience Points)
- Earn XP by completing lessons and activities
- XP helps you level up and unlock new content
- [Learn more about XP](../hearts-and-xp/xp-and-levels)

### Hearts
- Hearts represent your lives in the app
- You lose a heart when you make mistakes in lessons
- Hearts refill over time
- [Learn more about Hearts](../hearts-and-xp/hearts-system)

### Streaks
- Build a daily streak by using the app consistently
- Maintain your streak to earn rewards
- [Learn more about Streaks](../streaks/daily-streaks)

## 3. Set Up Notifications

Enable notifications to:
- Get daily reminders to practice
- Track your streak
- Receive motivational messages

**To enable:**
1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Notifications**
2. Toggle on "Daily Reminders"
3. Choose your preferred reminder time

## 4. Explore Features

### Lessons
Browse structured content organized by topic and difficulty.

### Challenges
Participate in daily and weekly challenges to earn bonus XP.

### Community
Connect with other learners and share your progress.

### Subscription (Optional)
Unlock premium content, remove ads, and get unlimited hearts.

## Tips for Success

1. **Practice daily** - Even 5 minutes helps maintain your streak
2. **Set realistic goals** - Start small and build consistency
3. **Review mistakes** - Learn from incorrect answers
4. **Explore different lessons** - Discover topics that resonate with you
5. **Join the community** - Connect with others on the same journey
