# Creating Your Account

Learn how to create a Faithflow account and get started.

## Sign Up Options

Faithflow offers multiple ways to create an account:

### Apple Sign-In (Recommended)
1. Tap "Continue with Apple" on the welcome screen
2. Authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password
3. Choose whether to share or hide your email
4. Complete the quick setup flow

### Email Sign-Up
1. Tap "Sign Up with Email"
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a secure password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile

## Account Setup

After creating your account, you'll:
1. Choose your display name
2. Set your learning goals (optional)
3. Select notification preferences
4. Complete a brief onboarding tutorial

## Troubleshooting

**Can't sign in with Apple?**
- Ensure you're signed into iCloud on your device
- Check that "Sign in with Apple" is enabled in Settings

**Didn't receive verification email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Request a new verification email from the app
