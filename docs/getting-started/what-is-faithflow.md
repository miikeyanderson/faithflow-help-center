# What is Faithflow?

Faithflow is a faith-based learning app designed to help you grow spiritually through structured lessons, engaging activities, and community features.

## Overview

Faithflow combines modern learning techniques with spiritual content to create an engaging and effective way to deepen your faith.

### Key Features

- **Structured Lessons**: Progress through carefully designed lessons that build on each other
- **XP & Levels**: Earn experience points and level up as you complete lessons
- **Hearts System**: Stay motivated with a lives system that refills over time
- **Daily Streaks**: Build consistency with daily practice and maintain your streak
- **Community**: Connect with other learners on the same journey
- **Subscriptions**: Access premium content and features (optional)

## How It Works

1. **Create an Account**: Sign up with your email or Apple ID
2. **Choose Your Path**: Select lessons that interest you
3. **Learn Daily**: Complete lessons to earn XP and maintain your streak
4. **Track Progress**: Monitor your growth through levels and achievements
5. **Stay Engaged**: Participate in challenges and community features

## Who Is Faithflow For?

Faithflow is designed for anyone looking to:
- Deepen their spiritual understanding
- Build consistent daily practices
- Learn at their own pace
- Connect with a community of faith
