# Contact Support

Need help with Faithflow? We're here to assist you.

## Before Contacting Support

Please check:
1. **Search this Help Center**: Your question might already be answered
2. **Check [Common Issues](./common-issues)**: See if your problem has a known solution
3. **Update the App**: Make sure you're running the latest version

## How to Reach Us

### Send Feedback (In-App)
The fastest way to get help:
1. Open Faithflow
2. Go to **Settings** → **Support & Feedback**
3. Tap **Send Feedback**
4. Describe your issue in detail

### Email Support
You can also email us directly at:  
**[support email - to be added]**

When contacting us, please include:
- Your Faithflow account email
- Device type and iOS version
- App version (found in Settings → About)
- Description of the issue
- Screenshots (if applicable)

## Response Times

- **General Inquiries**: 1-2 business days
- **Technical Issues**: 24-48 hours
- **Billing Questions**: 24 hours

## What to Include

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please provide:

### For Technical Issues
- What were you doing when the problem occurred?
- Does it happen every time or intermittently?
- Have you tried restarting the app?
- Any error messages you saw

### For Billing Issues
- Purchase receipt or transaction ID
- Date of purchase
- Expected vs. actual charge

### For Account Issues
- Account email address
- When the account was created
- Specific issue (login, password, data sync, etc.)
