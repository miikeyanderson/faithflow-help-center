# Understanding XP & Levels

Learn how experience points (XP) and levels work in Faithflow.

## What is XP?

XP (Experience Points) measures your learning progress in Faithflow. Earn XP by completing lessons, answering questions, and participating in challenges.

## How to Earn XP

- **Complete Lessons**: Earn XP for each lesson finished
- **Correct Answers**: Bonus XP for accuracy
- **Daily Practice**: Consistency bonuses
- **Challenges**: Special events and challenges
- **Perfect Scores**: Extra XP for flawless completion

## Levels

As you earn XP, you level up:
- Each level requires more XP than the last
- Leveling up unlocks new content and features
- Higher levels show your dedication and progress

## XP Dashboard

View your XP stats in the app:
- Total XP earned
- Current level and progress to next level
- XP breakdown by activity type
- Leaderboard ranking (if enabled)
