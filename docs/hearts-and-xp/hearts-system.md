# Hearts System

Understanding how hearts work and how to manage them.

## What are Hearts?

Hearts represent your lives in Faithflow. You start with a full set of hearts and lose them when you make mistakes during lessons.

## Losing Hearts

You lose a heart when you:
- Answer a question incorrectly
- Skip required activities
- Fail to complete a lesson section

## Refilling Hearts

Hearts refill automatically over time:
- One heart refills every few hours
- All hearts refill overnight
- Subscribers get unlimited hearts or faster refill rates

## Managing Your Hearts

**Tips to preserve hearts:**
- Read lesson content carefully
- Take your time on questions
- Review before answering
- Use hints when available

**When you run out:**
- Wait for hearts to refill
- Practice with heart-free review mode
- Subscribe for unlimited hearts
