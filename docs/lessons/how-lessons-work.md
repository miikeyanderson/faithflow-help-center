# How Lessons Work

Understanding the structure and flow of Faithflow lessons.

## Lesson Structure

Each lesson in Faithflow includes:
- **Introduction** - Overview of the topic
- **Core Content** - Main teaching material
- **Practice Activities** - Interactive exercises
- **Review** - Summary and key takeaways

## Lesson Types

### Reading Lessons
Learn through structured reading material with comprehension checks.

### Interactive Lessons
Engage with activities, questions, and multimedia content.

### Reflection Lessons
Guided journaling and personal reflection exercises.

## Earning XP

You earn XP by:
- Completing lesson activities
- Answering questions correctly
- Finishing entire lessons
- Achieving perfect scores

## Losing Hearts

Hearts are lost when you:
- Answer questions incorrectly
- Skip required activities
- Exit lessons without saving progress

## Progress Tracking

Your progress is automatically saved:
- Resume lessons where you left off
- View completion percentage
- Track your learning history
