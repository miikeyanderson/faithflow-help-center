# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 6, 2025

## Introduction

Asymmetric Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Faithflow mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and profile information when you create an account
- **User Content**: Progress data, lesson completions, and preferences you choose to save
- **Communication Data**: Feedback, support requests, and communications with us

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: App features used, lessons completed, session duration, and interaction patterns
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Analytics Data**: Crash reports, performance metrics, and aggregated usage statistics

### Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect information:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication services
- **Analytics Services**: To understand app usage and improve performance
- **Crash Reporting**: To identify and fix technical issues

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the App
- Create and manage your account
- Track your learning progress and personalize your experience
- Send you notifications, reminders, and updates (with your consent)
- Respond to your support requests and feedback
- Analyze app usage to improve features and performance
- Ensure security and prevent fraud

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not sell** your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Service Providers**: Third-party vendors who assist in app operations (e.g., Supabase for data storage)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Usage Data**: Retained for analytical purposes, typically for 12-24 months
- **Deleted Account**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics or marketing communications
- **Data Portability**: Export your progress and profile data

To exercise these rights, contact us at [support email - to be added].

## Children's Privacy

Faithflow is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted data storage
- Secure authentication practices
- Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Notifications and Permissions

The App may request permissions for:
- **Notifications**: To send reminders and updates (can be disabled in Settings)
- **Analytics**: To improve app performance (can be opted out in Settings)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via:
- In-app notifications
- Email (if you've provided one)
- Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

## International Users

Faithflow is operated from the United States. Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.
